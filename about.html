More about our family

Bangladesh Student Association of Brooklyn College

Bangladesh Student Association is a platform where all Bangladeshi students get together to celebrate our Bangladeshi culture and tradition on the campus of Brooklyn College.

Through that, we get an opportunity to socialize, make friends, have a diverting campus life, make timeless treasures of memories, usually by arranging events like the "Bengali New Year" or "Bangladesh Victory Day".

Besides that, we also get academic help and advisement from each other. Leaders and members of this family have taken the time to know more about our traditions, practice culture in order to lead this family which makes them eager to learn about leadership, responsibilities, time management, event management, networking, public speaking, and so on. By joining our club, you will not lose anything instead you will gain a lot of things.

Benefits of joining our Organization

Your success will be our happiness

Family

If you are coming to Brooklyn College you will have a home and If you join BSA Club you will have a family. We really mean it.

Friendship

More or less everyone experiences loneliness in the first year of college but BSA members always got each other’s back.

Culture

A platform where you will be comfortable to showcase your hidden talents. Singing, Dancing, Acting, Hosting, you name it, we’ll embrace it.

Networking

When it comes to Networking: "It's Not About Who You Know, It's About Who Knows You" Quality people matter to your academic journey.

Help & Advisement

"If you want to go somewhere, find someone who already been there" Definitely you can find someone experienced or taking the same path as you in our family.

Enjoyable Campus Life

Since we created BSA, our members always had quality time on campus. Our office room is decorated with ethnicity which can be your comfort zone any time as most of the time someone is there to hangout.

Leadership

Members who led this platform became leaders in the future. Organization management, communication, planning and executing, and a positive mindset will train you to be a good leader. You can have the opportunity to lead this family.

Professionalism

Teamworks, Interacting with members and officers, Room Booking, Meeting Arrangement, Time Management, Event Management, Public Speaking makes one professional. These are the real-life experiences our leaders & members get from this platform.
